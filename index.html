Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to common questions about our verification process.

Why do I need to go through verification?

Verification ensures that your qualifications and experience are accurately represented, making you a more attractive candidate to employers.

How long will the verification process take?

The verification process usually takes between 5-7 business days, depending on the complexity of the checks..

Can I update my information after verification?

Yes, you can update your information after verification. Simply contact our support team with the changes you'd like to make.

What happens if my background check fails?

If your background check fails, you will be notified via email with details about the specific issue. You can then take steps to address any discrepancies or provide additional documentation as needed.

Do I need to pay for the verification process?

No, the verification process is completely free for all users.

Will my personal data be kept secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal data. All information is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent.

What happens after I successfully verify?

Once your verification is complete, you will receive a confirmation email stating that your profile has been successfully verified. At this point, your profile will be considered ready for job opportunities, and you will be eligible to apply for open positions through EZhire. Verified candidates are given priority and will be referred to employers for relevant job openings based on your skills and qualifications. Additionally, you will gain access to exclusive job listings and increased visibility to potential employers, helping you move one step closer to your ideal career!
